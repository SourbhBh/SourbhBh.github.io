About Me

I am a PhD student in the ECE department at Cornell University, jointly advised by Jayadev Acharya and Aaron Wagner .
My research focuses broadly on problems in information theory and machine learning. Recently, I have been exploring the intersection of causality and machine learning, specifically causal discovery and causal representation learning.

Before coming to Cornell, I graduated with a BTech and MTech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Madras and was fortunate to be advised by Andrew Thangaraj.
